Guidelines for Figures

  • Number figures consecutively (Figure 1, Figure 2, etc.)
  • Place caption below the figure
  • Submit high-resolution images (minimum 300 DPI)
  • Accepted formats: PNG, JPEG, TIFF

Guidelines for Tables

  • Number tables consecutively (Table 1, Table 2, etc.)
  • Place caption above the table
  • Tables should be editable (not images)
  • Use horizontal lines only (no vertical lines)

Abstract

Maximum Word Count: 250 words

The abstract should provide a concise summary of the research including:

  • Background and objectives
  • Methods used
  • Key findings
  • Main conclusions

Main Body Structure

The manuscript should follow this standard structure:

1 Introduction
2 Material and Method
3 Results
4 Discussion
5 Conclusion
6 Competing Interest
7 Acknowledgement
8 Authors' Contributions

Statistical & Validity Requirements

Authors should provide appropriate statistical analysis to support their findings. The following are recommended:

Recommended Statistical Tests & Tools

  • ANOVA: For comparing means across multiple groups
  • SPSS: For comprehensive statistical analysis
  • Regression Analysis: For examining relationships between variables
  • Correlation Analysis: For measuring the strength of associations
  • t-test: For comparing means between two groups
  • Chi-square test: For categorical data analysis

Reporting Format

  • Report exact p-values (e.g., p = 0.023) instead of ranges
  • Include effect sizes where applicable
  • Provide confidence intervals for key estimates
  • Clearly state the statistical software and version used
  • Include sample size calculations for experimental studies

Submission Procedure

Follow these steps to submit your manuscript through our website:

1

Prepare Your Abstract

Write your abstract (250 words max) with 3-4 keywords and a 50-word bio-note.

2

Email Abstract for Initial Review

Send your abstract to jmri.siesascn@sies.edu.in for preliminary evaluation.

3

Await Abstract Acceptance

You will receive an acceptance decision within 15 days of submission.

4

Pay Processing Fee

After abstract acceptance, pay the processing fee of INR 500/-.

5

Submit Full Paper

Click the "Submit Manuscript" button on the website and fill out the submission form with all required details and upload your complete manuscript.

6

Peer Review Process

Your manuscript will undergo blind peer review. You may be asked to make revisions based on reviewer feedback.

7

Final Acceptance & Publication

Once approved, your paper will be published in the upcoming issue of the journal.
