Detailed Submission Guidelines

Comprehensive information for preparing your manuscript

Abstract Requirements

  • Word limit: 250-300 words
  • Include 3-4 keywords
  • Submit with 50 words bio-note
  • Email to: sies@ascn.edu.in
  • Processing fee of INR 500/- after abstract selection

Full Paper Format

  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman
  • Style: Latest APA style format
  • Originality: Paper must be original and unpublished
  • Simultaneous Submissions: Not allowed
  • Undertaking: Originality declaration required
  • Images: High resolution images only
  • Copyright: Required for all images/photos used

Length & Keywords

  • Page Count: Minimum 10 pages, Maximum 15 pages
  • Keywords: 3‚Äì4 keywords required

Referencing Style

All papers must follow the latest APA style format

Ensure all citations and references comply with current APA guidelines

Author Responsibility

  • Ensure all citations are correct and complete
  • No copyrighted content without proper permission
  • No unlawful or unethical content
  • Verify accuracy of all references

üí° Important:

All submissions must use the latest APA style format. Incorrect citation style may delay the review process.

Tables

  • Number tables consecutively (Table 1, Table 2, etc.)
  • Include a clear, descriptive title above the table
  • Use horizontal lines only (no vertical lines)
  • Place notes below the table if needed
  • Ensure tables are editable (not images)

Figures

  • Number figures consecutively (Figure 1, Figure 2, etc.)
  • Include a caption below the figure
  • Submit in high resolution (minimum 300 DPI)
  • Accepted formats: PNG, JPEG, TIFF
  • Ensure all text in figures is legible
  • Cite the source if not original

üìä Best Practice:

Place tables and figures close to where they are first mentioned in the text.

Originality & Plagiarism Policy

  • All papers must be original and unpublished
  • Authors must provide an undertaking of originality
  • No simultaneous submissions to other journals
  • Plagiarism must not exceed 10% - All submissions undergo plagiarism detection
  • Plagiarism checks will be conducted using standard detection software
  • Manuscripts exceeding 10% plagiarism will result in immediate rejection

Author Information Required

  • Full name of all authors
  • Academic qualifications
  • Institutional affiliation
  • Current designation/position
  • Contact information (email and phone)

Copyright & Image Rights

  • Authors must procure copyrights for all images/photos used
  • Only high resolution images are acceptable
  • Proof of copyright/permission for images must be provided
  • Authors retain copyright while granting publication rights to the journal
  • Proper attribution required for all borrowed visual content

All papers must be original with proper undertaking. Authors must procure copyrights for images and submit high-resolution files only.
