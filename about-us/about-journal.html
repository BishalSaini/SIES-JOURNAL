About the Journal

A peer-reviewed, multidisciplinary platform committed to advancing knowledge and innovation

SIES Journal of Multidisciplinary Research and Innovation

Bridging Disciplines, Fostering Innovation

The SIES Journal of Multidisciplinary Research and Innovation is a premier academic publication that provides a platform for researchers, scholars, and practitioners to share original research, innovative ideas, and critical analyses across diverse fields of study. Published by SIES College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Nerul, the journal upholds the highest standards of academic rigor and scholarly excellence.

Our journal embraces a multidisciplinary approach, welcoming contributions from the arts, sciences, commerce, technology, social sciences, and humanities. We believe that the most significant breakthroughs often occur at the intersection of disciplines, where diverse perspectives converge to address complex challenges facing society today.

Our Mission

To advance knowledge and innovation by providing a credible platform for disseminating high-quality, peer-reviewed research that addresses contemporary issues and contributes to academic discourse across multiple disciplines.

Our Vision

To become a leading multidisciplinary journal recognized globally for publishing transformative research that bridges theory and practice, fosters innovation, and contributes meaningfully to society's advancement.
