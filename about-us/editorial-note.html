Editorial Note

A message from the editorial team on our vision and commitment

Editorial Note

Dr. Koel Roychoudhury

Chief Editor

SIES Journal of Multidisciplinary Research & Innovation

Welcome to the inaugural issue of the SIES Journal of Multidisciplinary Research & Innovation (SIES JMRI)

A peer-reviewed academic research journal committed to advancing high-quality scholarly research across varied disciplines. This journal has been established with a clear vision to nurture rigorous academic inquiry, interdisciplinary collaboration, and wide dissemination of innovative research findings from research scholars, industry experts and academicians across the globe.

Our Commitment to Excellence

SIES JMRI embraces a double-blind peer-review process to ensure the integrity, validity, and excellence of all published work. Through this commitment, we aim to provide authors with constructive and unbiased feedback, while offering readers access to well-substantiated and impactful research.

Volume 1, Issue 1

As we launch Volume 1, Issue 1, we are proud to present a platform that not only supports emerging and established researchers but also promotes open access and wide visibility so that valuable insights can reach the academic community.

Acknowledgments

As we present this issue, we extend our heartfelt gratitude to the members of the Editorial Board, peer reviewers, and all contributing for their original research. Your collective efforts have laid the foundation for what we trust will be a sustained tradition of impactful and high-quality scholarly publication.

Join Our Academic Community

We invite readers to explore the research presented in this issue and to contribute to future volumes through the submission of original research. We aim to build an inclusive academic forum that values quality scholarship and diverse perspectives.

"With dedication to academic excellence and innovation, we embark on this journey to advance knowledge and foster scholarly discourse across disciplines."

Dr. Koel Roychoudhury

Chief Editor
