About the SIES Journal of Multidisciplinary Research

The SIES JMRI is a peer-reviewed, double-blind, multi-disciplinary annual online research journal (ISSN: 3048-9768 Online). It aims to bring interdisciplinary perspectives in social sciences to reflect on varied paradigms, perspectives, comprehensions, and contemplations.

Mission & Quality Focus

The journal continues the institution's legacy of fostering a research culture. We focus on promoting quality research globally, ensuring sustainable development. Our robust peer-review process ensures world-class articles that enrich the humanities.

About SIES College of Arts, Science & Commerce (Autonomous)

The South Indian Education Society (SIES) College of Arts, Science & Commerce (Autonomous) is a premier educational institution located in Nerul, Navi Mumbai. Established with the vision of providing quality higher education, the college has grown to become one of the most respected institutions in the region.

The college is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and has earned autonomous status from the UGC (2023-2024), allowing it to design innovative curricula and maintain academic flexibility while ensuring quality education that meets global standards.
