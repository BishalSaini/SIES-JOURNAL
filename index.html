Detailed Author Guidelines

Understand the requirements for manuscript preparation, ethical standards, and review timelines.

Manuscript Preparation

All submissions must be in MS Word, use Times New Roman, font size 12, with 1.5 spacing. The article length should be between 3000 and 6000 words (excluding abstract and references).

Referencing Style

The journal exclusively follows the latest edition of the **MLA** (Modern Language Association) citation and referencing style for all submissions.

Research Ethics

All research must comply with the SIESASCN Research Ethics Policy. Articles must be original, plagiarism-free, and authors must declare no conflict of interest.

Review Timeline

The initial review decision is typically communicated within 6 to 8 weeks. Review is double-blind peer-reviewed.
