SIES JMRI Journal Officially Launched

September 10, 2025 ISSN Certified
MILESTONE

We are proud to announce the official launch of SIES Journal of Multidisciplinary Research and Innovation (ISSN: 3048-9768), a peer-reviewed annual publication focusing on interdisciplinary research. The journal aims to bridge various academic disciplines and promote collaborative research.

Journal Features:

  • • ISSN: 3048-9768 (Online)
  • • Publication Frequency: Annual
  • • Review Process: Double-blind peer review
  • • Focus Areas: Multidisciplinary research across all academic fields
  • • Access Model: Open access, no publication charges
