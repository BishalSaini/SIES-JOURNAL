Quick Submission Guidelines

  • Abstract: 250-300 words with 3-4 keywords + 50 words bio-note
  • Format: MS Word (.docx), Times New Roman, Font 12 (Latest APA style)
  • Length: 3,000-5,000 words (8-10 pages)
  • Originality: Must be original, unpublished, no simultaneous submissions
  • Images: High resolution only, copyright required
  • Fee: INR 500/- processing fee after abstract selection

Submission Process: Submit abstract to jmri.siesascn@sies.edu.in. After acceptance (within 15 days), pay INR 500/-, then submit full paper. Full guidelines
